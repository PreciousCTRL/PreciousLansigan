TV Series

     My favorite TV shows span a variety of genres, but each one has left a lasting impression on me. All of Us Are Dead kept me on the edge of my seat with its thrilling take on the zombie apocalypse, while Narcos gave me an intense, gripping look into the world of drug cartels. Dark (Netflix) is one of the best mind-bending series I’ve watched, with its complex timeline and mysterious plot twists. I’m also a huge fan of Kingdom, the zombie-filled historical drama that brings a unique spin to the genre. Rick and Morty brings in tons of humor and wild sci-fi adventures, while Stranger Things never fails to captivate with its nostalgic 80s vibe and supernatural elements. Lastly, Avatar: The Last Airbender holds a special place in my heart as a masterpiece of animation with unforgettable characters and life lessons. These shows always manage to hook me in, whether I’m in the mood for action, mystery, or something lighter!
