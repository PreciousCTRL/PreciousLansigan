My Beats

Alternative/Indie HipHop/Rap Metal

     My music taste is a unique blend of genres, where I gravitate toward alternative/indie sounds, hip hop/rap, and pop music, with a bit of metal and R&B/soul thrown in. I love the experimental, boundary-pushing elements of artists like Tyler, The Creator and Joji, but I also appreciate the raw energy of metal bands like System of a Down and Metallica. My hip hop preferences are diverse, from the introspective lyricism of Kendrick Lamar to the more eclectic styles of Doechii and Joost Klein. I’m also drawn to the soulful, jazzy vibe of artists like Laufey. This mix of genres reflects my love for both creative exploration and emotional depth in music.
