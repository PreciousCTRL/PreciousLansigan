Germany

Dark was filmed in various locations in Germany, with several iconic sites serving as key locations in the show. While the town of Winden is fictional, the church, school, and power plant in the series were filmed at real locations. Visiting these sites would allow any Dark fan to experience the haunting world of the show.

Places I want to Visit in Germany:

  • St. Christopher's Church in Buchschlag: The church, featured prominently in the series, was filmed at the St. Christopher's Church in Buchschlag, a district of the town of Dreieich. The church’s exterior appears in several scenes, including those featuring the characters of Jonas and Martha.
  • Gymnasium in Babenhausen: The school where many of the characters attend in Dark was filmed at the Gymnasium in Babenhausen, a small town in Hesse. The school plays a major role in the show, particularly for the younger characters who experience strange events linked to time travel.
  • Nuclear Power Plant in Biblis: The nuclear power plant, featured as a key location in the show, is the real-life Biblis Nuclear Power Plant. It is one of the most important locations in the series, as the plant is central to the mysterious events surrounding time travel and the opening of the wormhole in Dark.

To get there:

  • Schengen Visa
  • Airplane Ticket
  • Bus/Train Ticket
