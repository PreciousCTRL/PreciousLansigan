Rotterdam

The Netherlands

Rotterdam is a city that has always fascinated me. It's a place where modern architecture meets history, and I can't wait to see it all. I've always wanted to visit because of the amazing landmarks, vibrant culture, and the unique vibe of the city.

Places I want to Visit in Rotterdam:

  • Erasmus Bridge (De Zwaan): This bridge is a symbol of Rotterdam, known for its beautiful design and impressive size. I can't wait to walk across it and enjoy the view of the city.
  • Markthal: A food lover's paradise, the Markthal is a beautiful indoor market where I can explore fresh local food and cuisine. It sounds like the perfect place to indulge in Rotterdam's food culture.
  • Boijmans Van Beuningen Museum: For anyone who loves art, this museum is a must-visit. It has a rich collection of art and offers a chance to explore Rotterdam’s cultural heritage.

To get there:

  • Schengen Visa
  • Airplane Ticket - to Amsterdam
  • Bus/Train Ticket - to Rotterdam
