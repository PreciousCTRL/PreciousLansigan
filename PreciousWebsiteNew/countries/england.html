England

     I've always been a huge fan of the BBC's Sherlock with Benedict Cumberbatch. The show’s filming locations in London and around England bring Sherlock Holmes and Dr. Watson's world to life. I’d love to visit these places and experience the iconic spots from the series firsthand.

Places I want to Visit in England:

  • 221B Baker Street: Perhaps the most iconic location, this address is the fictional home of Sherlock Holmes and Dr. Watson. The exterior of 221B is located in London, and though it’s not the exact address used in the show, it’s the one most closely associated with the famous detective.
  • Speedy's Cafe (North Gower Street): Located right next to the filming location for 221B Baker Street, Speedy’s Cafe is a fan-favorite location from the show. It’s where Sherlock and Watson are often seen grabbing a quick meal and discussing their cases. The cafe still retains its fame with fans of the series.
  • The Royal Courts of Justice: Used in several episodes, the Royal Courts of Justice in London features in scenes where Sherlock is involved in legal cases. It is often shown as a backdrop to some of the more dramatic moments in the series.

How to get there?:

  • You must acquire UK Visa
  • Airplane Ticket to England
  • Bus/Train Ticket to your destinations
